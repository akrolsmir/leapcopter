Introducing the LeapCopter!

An RC helicopter controlled by hand gestures. Open source.

Promo Video

The Hack

  1. Get a copy of Leap Motion's The Leap. (Or four.)

  2. Impulsively run out and buy an RC helicopter.

  3. Disassemble the RC controller and expose the potentiometers.

  4. Mechanically attach (aka superglue) servomotors to the potentiometers.

  5. Wire up an Arduino to the servomotors.

  6. Communicate between The Leap and the Arduino with Python.

  7. (In progress) Weaponize >:)
